❌ Original iPhone Timer

The current design with usability issues

Current iPhone Timer Interface

Problems with the Original Design:

  • Requires lots of scrolling - Setting 45 minutes means scrolling through 45 numbers
  • Easy to overshoot - The wheel picker is hard to stop exactly where you want
  • No quick presets - Common times like 5, 10, 15 minutes require manual scrolling every time
  • Time consuming - Takes 15-20 seconds to set a simple timer
  • Precision issues - Difficult to see exact selection on the picker wheel
